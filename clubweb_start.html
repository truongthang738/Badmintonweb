Badminton Club, Davenport West High School

Practice Hours:

Every Sunday (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM) and Monday (7:00 PM - 10:00 PM) from January 28 to April 30, except spring break (March 24 - April 1)

Membership Fee: $25/academic year

All players have to bring their own badminton rackets and feather shuttlecocks which we sell at $22/tube. One can rent badminton rackets for free from the locker room as well.

If you are interested, click on "Join" button above and see Membership Requirements below (under Additional Information.)

If you have any questions or concerns, you may direct them to truongthang738@gmail.com

The Badminton Club maintains an external website that provides more information on badminton tournaments, equipment, club events and photo galleries.

The primary focus of the club is to enable competition: the club provides high grade shuttlecocks at low cost and encourages tournament play by way of generous reimbursements.
